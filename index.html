Destiny 2 Mythic Challenge

What is the Mythic Challenge?

The Destiny 2 Mythic Challenge is the most hardcore way to play Destiny 2. In addition to the base game's rules, there are a few added restrictions to the way you are allowed to play. These restrictions make the game much more difficult, and are meant to test the gameplay capabilities of well versed and experienced players.
